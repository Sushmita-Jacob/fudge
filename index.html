What exactly is fudge?

Fudge is a type of soft candy. It is usually made with sugar and milk and resembles a chocolatey flavor. While it is similar to a brownie, fudge has a more chewy texture while brownies are more cake-like.

What is Fudge Fudge Fudge?

Fudge Fudge Fudge is a YSWS. Ship a project, and get fudge delieved right to your door!

What's a YSWS?

A YSWS, an acronym for "You Ship, We Ship," is a program by Hack Club where high schoolers can "ship" (make their project open-source and publically accessible) a technical project for prizes! Acceptable projects can include games, websites, apps, chat bots, hardware, and more! Prizes are dependent on the specific YSWS program.

How much fudge will we get?

For every three (3) hours of code, you will receive a half (1/2) pound of fudge. For every six (6) hours of code, you will receieve one (1) pound of fudge. This can continue for an infinite amount of hours until the fudge is sold out.

What are the requirements for Fudge Fudge Fudge?

You must:

  • Be a high schooler, aged 13-18 (inclusive)
  • Create a technical project
  • Track the time spent on your project with Hackatime
  • Open source your project on a website such as GitHub
What is Hackatime?

Hackatime is a time-tracking extension available on most major coding editors. It is used to verify how much time was spent on a project and helps check for fraud.

Where does this fudge come from?

The fudge is from the Shelburne County Store, a small shop in Vermont, near Hack Club's Headquarters (HQ).

Who is in charge of Fudge Fudge Fudge?

Reem from Hack Club is in charge of Fudge Fudge Fudge!

What is Hack Club?

Hack Club is a 501(c)(3) non-profit organization aiming to encourage teens to learn coding and provides a community for high schoolers interested in building technial projects.
